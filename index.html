Information

Forrest Gump is a simple man from Alabama with a low IQ, but his heart is pure and kind. Despite his limitations, Forrest finds himself in the middle of many major historical events in America during the 20th century all without really trying. From becoming a college football star, serving bravely in the Vietnam War, starting a successful shrimp business, to running across the country for years, Forrest unintentionally influences the world around him. Throughout his journey, Forrest never stops loving Jenny Curran, his childhood friend, whose life is troubled and full of struggles. Although their paths often diverge, Forrest's unwavering love for Jenny remains at the heart of the story.The film is both heartwarming and emotional, blending humor with touching moments. It reminds us that sometimes, extraordinary lives can come from the most unexpected people.
